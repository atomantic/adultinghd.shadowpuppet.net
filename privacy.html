Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Data Storage

ADultingHD stores all your data locally on your device in the app's Documents directory. Your tasks, profile, completions, and settings are saved as JSON files that never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export them.

Data We Do Not Collect

ADultingHD does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Health or fitness data

In-App Purchases

ADultingHD offers an optional one-time Pro unlock via Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. For information about how Apple handles purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

ADultingHD may send local notifications (daily reminders and task due alerts) if you grant permission. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and are not routed through any external server.

Third-Party Services

ADultingHD does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs, pixels, or external scripts in the app.

No Account Required

ADultingHD does not require any account creation, login, or authentication. You can use the full app without providing any personal information.

Data Export

You can export your data as a JSON backup file at any time from the app's Settings. This file is stored locally and shared only through methods you choose (AirDrop, Files, etc.).

Children's Privacy

ADultingHD does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no social features, user-generated content sharing, or external communication mechanisms.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in Settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@shadowpuppet.net.
